How to join listserv:

  • Send the following command via electronic mail to listproc@cornell.edu. Type (firstname) (lastname) where (firstname) is your first name and (lastname) is your last name.
  • For questions or additional information, contact Marisa Alcorta at mfa4@cornell.edu or Tom Smulders at tvs1@cornell.edu.

How to join club:

  • Look at upcoming dates on the calendar for opportunities to meet the team as well as workshops and other dance events to become familiar with the dance style.
  • To subscribe to our weekly announcements, send an email to ballroom-l-request@cornell.edu with the body of the message saying "join" in plain text.

Contact us: @CornellBallroomClub on Facebook
